About DataGaffer

DataGaffer is an independent football analytics platform providing realistic match simulations, player performance projections, and statistical insights for leagues and competitions worldwide.

Our mission is to help fans and bettors make data-driven decisions using transparent, up-to-date, and accurate simulations. All projections are refreshed daily and are based on recent team performance, historical statistics, and league-adjusted modeling.
